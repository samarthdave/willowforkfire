Description of Service Area

To determine if your specific address is within the service boundaries, please check your Fort Bend County Property Tax Statement or visit the Fort Bend County Central Appraisal District website. Properties served by the Willowfork Fire Department are taxed by Fort Bend County ESD#2 which has an Entity Code of "R41" on the Central Appraisal District website: www.fbcad.org. Click to view PDF Map of Emergency Service District #2

Willowfork Fire Department covers the following communities:

  • Avalon @ Seven Meadows
  • Cinco Ranch East (south of Fort Bend/Harris County line, north of Buffalo Bayou and west of Kelliwood)
  • Cinco Ranch West
  • Cinco Ranch Southwest (north of Little Prong Creek)
  • Falcon Ranch
  • Grand Lakes (west of Little Prong Creek)
  • Grayson Lakes
  • Heritage Grand
  • Katy Creek Ranch
  • Kings Lake
  • Lake Pointe Estates
  • Mills Pointe
  • Parkway Oaks
  • Pine Mill Ranch
  • Seven Meadows (north of Little Prong Creek)
  • Silver Ranch
